Delete Your Data

Last updated: April 11, 2026

How to delete your account and data

You can permanently delete your Kiron account and all associated data directly from within the app:

  1. Open the Kiron app and log in
  2. Tap the Settings tab
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account
  4. Confirm deletion when prompted

This will immediately and permanently delete:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All workout programs and plans
  • All workout history and session logs
  • All set and exercise logs
  • Your profile and personal information (age, weight, height)
  • Your app preferences

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, you can request deletion by emailing us at help@trykiron.com with the subject line "Delete my account" and the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What data is retained

Once deleted, no personal data is retained. Aggregated, anonymous analytics data (which cannot be linked back to you) may be retained for product improvement purposes.

Questions

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at help@trykiron.com.
